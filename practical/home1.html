Library

    Synonymous with the abode of knowledge, library of ABESEC is stocked with around 1.14 lac books, research journals,
    reports, multi-volume reference works, dictionaries, encyclopedias and handbooks to propel the academic and research
    growth of the students and faculty members. The library is periodically re-equipped with all the printed and
    e-resources from diverse streams, to provide for the most recent tools, to support teaching, learning and research
    activities. The library also facilitates access to a number of e-journals and ebooks through its participation in
    Nalanda E-consortia(AKTU).

    The library is instrumental to support information literacy and profound learning skills.
